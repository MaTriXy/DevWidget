Android app widget for developers to have control over their apps really quickly on their homescreen.

Features include:
- Launching
- Uninstalling
- Go to app details
- Clear data
- Deeplink launching (Coming soon)
- Automated clear data (Coming soon)

The application is open source and the source can be found here:
https://github.com/tasomaniac/DevWidget/
